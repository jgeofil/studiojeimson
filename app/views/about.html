Design, Live visuals, Media theory.

Since the founding of my practice back in 2012 I have worked for and together with different organizations and entrepreneurs to help build brands and push the envelope when it comes to visual communication, be it online, interactive or offline.

After having graduated (Willem de Kooning Academy, BDes) in 2014 I have enjoyed expanding my professional interests:

  • Two days of the week I work at NNF Productions, a young production office that creates several festivals and events of different sizes in and around Rotterdam, showcasing a wide range of (audiovisual) artists and striving to be at the forefront of the dance scene.
  • Since two years I’ve been working closely together with SEKOIA, an on- and offline platform for and about electronic music, media production and dance events. My part in this team is to develop SEKOIA’s visual identity by creating new media products and visually supporting performing artists (as the resident VJ) at events hosted by us.
  • On the weekends and at events I am a host and bartender at Gallery de Garage Rotterdam, where I enjoy learning about and presenting contemporary art, and serve nice cold beers of course.
  • Last but definitely not least I work on an ongoing research project about how social platforms facilitate and shape contemporary visual culture. Earlier this year I presented this research as a lecture + VJ presentation at the city theatre of Amsterdam. I continue this research aside from other activities in hopes of spreading the word and hopefully publishing soon.

In my work I value getting to know my clients. It's a thorough briefing, getting to the core of the clients ambition, that ignites my passion for creativity. Design is the practice I use to help entrepreneurs and businesses that appeal to me achieve their goals. In this way I embrace the aspect of providing a service while maintaining the position of a specialist and advisor that develops strategies and translates these into specific visual languages.

Each and every final design is the logical outcome of a visual and intuitive but mostly conceptual process. Therefor I never limit my concepts with my technical knowhow. This results in me learning new techniques or collaborating with specialists. It made me strive to avoid any personal style when it comes to aesthetics.

Each project is a custom job, my approach is my signature.
