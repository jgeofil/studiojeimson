Inanimate Life on Tumblr
Client: Self initiated


Physical set-up for shoot (mixed materials)

Inanimate Life on Tumblr is the first of a series of videos that follow the journey of a photograph through the network of TUmblr. The visual language I developed for the corporate identity of a new club night in Rotterdam shows my fascination and appreciation of online visual culture and the networked image. It celebrates the internet's low resolution images rather then discriminating against then. The idea of the traditional poster displaying a screenshot-like image plays on the differences and similarities between static and interactive media.
