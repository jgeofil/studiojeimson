Amsterdam Eats, Organic Food Factory - Corporate Identity and Campaign
Client: SFX Entertainment Company/ Warehouse Elementenstraat


DIN A1 Poster (mixed materials/ print)

" Amsterdam, city of food and music, welcomes a fresh food festival experience!
In October the Warehouse Elementenstraat opens it's doors for Amsterdam's first organic indoor food festival. Combining culinary delicacies with drinks and deep house music into a one of a kind happening, Amsterdam Eats is for everyone: a daylight filled indoor location with a familiar atmosphere, suitable for all kinds of friends and family happenings.

In developing the visual language for Amsterdam Eats I've experimented with more and less abstract references to the joys of eating and working with food as ingredients and material. Using a physical set up to play around with scale, lighting, and the actual subjects made way for a one of a kind aesthetic.
