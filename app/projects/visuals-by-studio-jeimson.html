Since I've started VJing mid 2014 (initially to expand SEKOIA's visual identity with a live performance) I've been thinking a lot about what I would want my sets to be like. I like to see this as an ongoing study with constantly changing content and experiments with software as well as hardware.

Drawing inspiration from theorists like Henry Jenkins, Clay Shirky, Jaron Lanier, Geert Lovink and artists LIL INTERNET and Ryoji Ikeda, DJ Girltalk and the documentary RIP: A Remix Manifesto, I will continue to explore the combination of performance: video + audio + venue + crowd chemistry from a media critical perspective for many years to come.

Check out my tumblr Club Compositing for a collection of stills and snippets from my VJ deck.
