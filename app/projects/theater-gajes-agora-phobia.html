Agora Phobia - Artwork for Sountrack
Client: Theater Gajes

Physical set-up for shoot (mixed materials)

Agora Phobia is a unique play which is performed in public space. The viewer is surrounded by actors, machines and live music as part of this experience. In the play, the public gradually becomes a part of a transforming construction site in which a conflict concerning city planning takes place.

As the story deals with tension between stakeholders with different levels of authority, I've created an image that shows a collision of perspectives. Experimenting with angles and ratio resulted in a game of scale, geometry, lighting and optical illusion.
